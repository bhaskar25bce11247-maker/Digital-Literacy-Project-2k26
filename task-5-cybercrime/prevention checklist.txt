Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist: 

General safety rules:

1. Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable 
two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  

2. Never share personal details like Aadhaar number, OTPs, or 
passwords with anyone online

3. Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into 
banking or important accounts on unsecured networks  

4. Always verify links before clicking—phishing scams often 
look like oAicial emails or messages  

5. Keep your phone and apps updated to protect against 
security vulnerabilities  
Financial / UPI Safety Tips 

6. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP, even if someone claims to 
be from your bank  

7. Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending 
money via apps like Google Pay or PhonePe  

8. Do not accept unknown “collect requests” or payment 
requests from strangers  

9.. Avoid scanning random QR codes, especially those shared 
by unknown sources  
If Something Goes Wrong 

10. Immediately report fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal 
(cybercrime.gov.in)  

11. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial 
fraud assistance
