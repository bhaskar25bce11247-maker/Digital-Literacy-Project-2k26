Social media checklist DO’s and DONT’s 


Do’s 

1. Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts  
2. Think before posting and ensure your content is appropriate  
3. Adjust privacy settings to protect your personal information  
4. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation  
5. Use social media positively for learning, networking, and collaboration  

Don’ts

1. Don’t share personal details like address, phone number, or passwords  
2. Don’t post oAensive, harmful, or inappropriate content  
3. Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown messages  
4. Don’t overshare your daily activities or location publicly  
5. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative online behaviour
